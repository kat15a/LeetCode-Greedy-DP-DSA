Problem: Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock II

Problem Statement:
You are given an integer array prices where prices[i]
is the price of a stock on the ith day.

You may complete as many transactions as you like
(buy one and sell one share multiple times).

However:
- You must sell the stock before you buy again.

Task:
Return the maximum profit you can achieve.

Approach:
- At each day we have two states:
  1. Buy state → we can buy the stock or skip.
  2. Sell state → we can sell the stock or skip.
- Use recursion with memoization to store results.
- DP state:
    dp[index][buy]

Transitions:
- Buy:
    max(-price + next sell state, skip)
- Sell:
    max(price + next buy state, skip)

Algorithm Used:
- Dynamic Programming
- Recursion + Memoization
- Stock DP Pattern

Time Complexity:
- O(N)

Space Complexity:
- O(N)
