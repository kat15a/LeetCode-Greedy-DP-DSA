Problem: Climbing Stairs

Problem Statement:
You are climbing a staircase with n steps.

Each time you can either climb:
- 1 step
- 2 steps

Task:
Return the number of distinct ways to reach the top.

Approach:
- At every step we have two choices:
    1. Take one step
    2. Take two steps
- The number of ways to reach step n is:
  
  ways(n) = ways(n-1) + ways(n-2)

- Use recursion with memoization to store previously computed results
  and avoid repeated calculations.

Algorithm Used:
- Recursion
- Dynamic Programming (Top-Down / Memoization)

Time Complexity:
- O(N)

Space Complexity:
- O(N)
