Problem: Painting the Walls

Problem Statement:
You are given two integer arrays cost and time of length n.

- cost[i] → cost of hiring the ith painter
- time[i] → number of additional walls that can be painted for free
            after hiring the ith painter

If you hire painter i:
- You pay cost[i]
- One wall is painted by the hired painter
- Additionally, time[i] walls are painted for free

Task:
Return the minimum cost required to paint all n walls.

Approach:
- At each painter we have two choices:
  1. Do not hire the painter → move to next painter.
  2. Hire the painter → reduce remaining walls by (1 + time[i])
     and add cost[i].
- Use recursion with memoization to compute the minimum cost.
- DP state:
    dp[index][remainingWalls]

Algorithm Used:
- Dynamic Programming
- Recursion + Memoization
- Knapsack Style Optimization

Time Complexity:
- O(N²)

Space Complexity:
- O(N²)
